LEGO Creator Expert Taj Mahal

The LEGO Creator Expert Taj Mahal is a highly detailed set that consists of over 5,900 pieces, making it one of the largest LEGO sets ever made. The set includes intricate architectural details such as minarets, domes, and arches, and comes with a display stand so you can show off your creation. It is recommended for ages 16 and up.

Barbie Dreamhouse

The Barbie Dreamhouse is a classic toy that has been updated for modern times. This three-story house has seven rooms, including a kitchen, living room, and bedroom, as well as a working elevator and a pool with a slide. It also features interactive lights, sounds, and motion-activated appliances. The set comes with over 70 accessories and is recommended for ages 3 and up.

Razor A Kick Scooter

The Razor A Kick Scooter is a classic toy that has been a favorite of kids and adults for over 20 years. It features a sturdy aluminum frame, 98mm inline-style wheels, and a rear fender brake. It is recommended for ages 5 and up and can support riders up to 143 pounds.
